Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard
Objective

To analyze retail sales data and identify key drivers of revenue, profitability, and the impact of discounting across categories and regions.

Tools Used
Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, PivotCharts, Dashboard Design)
Key Insights
High revenue does not guarantee profitability
Furniture generated the 2nd highest sales, but underperformed in profit due to excessive discounting
Technology is the most efficient category
Lower discount exposure resulted in the highest profit contribution
Critical discount threshold identified (~20%)
Profitability declines sharply beyond this point and becomes negative at higher discount levels

Business Risk
Over-reliance on aggressive discounting is eroding profit margins and creating unsustainable growth.
Recommendations
Limit discounting above 20% unless strategically justified
Focus growth on high-margin categories like Technology
Re-evaluate pricing strategy for Furniture to reduce margin erosion
