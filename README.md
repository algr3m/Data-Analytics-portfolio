Sales & Profitability Analysis
Objective

Analyze sales data to identify key drivers of revenue and profitability.

Key Insights
Furniture drives the 2nd-highest sales but the lowest profit
Technology generates the highest profit with lower discounting
Office supplies have the least sales but generate more profit than furniture
Profitability becomes negative beyond 20% discount
